Refund Policy


Updated: September 01, 2024

1. Full Refund Period
Customers are eligible for a full refund within [7-14] days of purchase, provided that no classes, workshops, or services have been used. This applies to all single class passes, memberships, and workshop registrations.

2. Non-Refundable Fees
Registration fees for workshops, retreats, or teacher training are non-refundable. In the case of cancellation, this fee will not be returned.

3. Cancellation Policy
For regular classes or workshops, customers must cancel at least [24-48] hours in advance to be eligible for a refund or to reschedule the session.
Cancellations made less than [24] hours before the class or failure to attend will result in the full charge for the session.

4. Memberships and Class Packages
For memberships (monthly or yearly), customers may cancel at any time and receive a pro-rated refund for the unused portion of the membership.
Class packages are non-refundable but can be transferred to another person or credited toward future services at the studio.

5. Special Events and Retreats
For special events, retreats, or teacher training, refunds are available only if canceled at least [60-90] days before the event. After this period, a partial refund may be offered based on the costs incurred by the studio.
Non-refundable deposits are required for retreat reservations.

6. Exceptions
In cases of medical emergencies or extenuating circumstances, refunds may be issued at the studio’s discretion. A doctor’s note or other documentation may be required.
